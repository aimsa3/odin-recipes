Stir Fry

Description

This recipe will tell you how to generally how to cook a chicken stir fry. You can use whatever sauce and other ingredients you want.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Egg noodles

Steps

  1. Heat oil in wok
  2. Fry chicken until cooked through
  3. While waiting for the chicken, soak noodles in hot water in a saucepan
  4. When chicken is done, add vegetables and cook for 4-5 minutes
  5. Add your sauce and cook for 2-3 minutes
  6. Drain the noodles
  7. Add noodles and cook for 2 minutes
  8. Serve!
