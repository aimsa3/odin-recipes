Pesto Pasta

Description

This recipe is for pesto pasta. I normally use green pesto, but you can make this with red pesto as well.

You can add whatever toppings you want, but I normally use parmesan and broccoli.

Ingredients

  • Any pasta you like
  • Parmesan or any cheese you want
  • Green or red pesto
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta until it's as soft as you want
  2. Start cooking any veg in another pan
  3. Drain the pasta with a colander
  4. Put the pasta back into the pan
  5. Mix in pesto until you're happy
  6. Drain the veg and mix in
  7. Mix in cheese
  8. Serve!
