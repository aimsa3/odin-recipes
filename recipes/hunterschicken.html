Hunter's Chicken

Description

This recipe is for hunter's chicken. Hunter's chicken consists of chicken wrapped in bacon and covered in BBQ sauce and cheese.

You can have whatever you want on the side but this recipe uses broccoli.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • 1 or 2 bacon rashers
  • BBQ sauce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius (fan)
  2. Cover chicken breast with bacon and cook for 15 minutes
  3. Cover chicken with BBQ sauce and then cover with cheddar cheese
  4. Put back in oven for another 15 minutes
  5. Chop broccoli
  6. Salt the water in the pan, and then start cooking the broccoli once the timer reaches 10 minutes
  7. Start on quite a high heat but turn it down as it starts to boil
  8. Serve!
